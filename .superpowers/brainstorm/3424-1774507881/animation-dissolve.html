15 Seconds Theme

Visual Style: Retro Digital / Neon

Animation Strategy

The numbers will be glowing neon outlines. We need to decide how the "3D movement" and "dissolve" look.

  • Depth: Numbers are stacked along the Z-axis.
  • Movement: As time passes, the whole stack shifts forward.
  • Dissolve: The front-most number needs to disappear.

Question 2: Dissolve Effect

For the "Retro Digital" style, what kind of dissolve feels most thematic?

A

Glitch / Flicker Out

The number flickers rapidly, perhaps with some horizontal scan-line displacement, then vanishes. Very "retro hardware".

B

Neon Vaporize

The neon lines break into small glowing particles that drift apart and fade. More organic/fluid.

C

Scanline Wipe

A horizontal line "erases" the number from top to bottom, leaving a brief trail. Precise and technical.
