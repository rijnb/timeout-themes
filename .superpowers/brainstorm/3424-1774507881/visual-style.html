15 Seconds Theme

Creating a 3D countdown timer theme

Theme Description

A new theme called "15 Seconds" that shows a countdown of 15 very large numbers.

  • Numbers appear in 3D space.
  • The front number (starting at 15) is very large and clear.
  • Other numbers (14, 13, ...) are behind it.
  • Numbers move to the front as the countdown continues.
  • Each number dissolves after one second as the next one comes forward.

Question 1: Visual Style

How should the "3D space" and the "numbers" look? Should they be solid, outlined, or have a specific texture/glow?

A

Modern Glass / Frosted

Semi-transparent numbers with a slight blur and soft glow. Feels premium and "spatial".

B

Retro Digital / Neon

Glowing neon outlines, like an old synthwave or cyberpunk interface. High contrast.

C

Solid & Bold

Heavy, solid typography with strong shadows to emphasize the 3D depth. Very clear and impactful.

D

Minimal Wireframe

Only the outlines of the numbers in 3D space. Very lightweight and architectural.
